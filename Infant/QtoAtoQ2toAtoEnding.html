Infancy

The next thing you know, there are sirens. Police and ambulances.
The car accident was not that serious.
Everyone had minor injuries - except the baby.
The baby girl/boy is dead. Not only do you have to deal with the grief of losing a child, you are under investigation
and this thought is forever in your brain:
had you put your baby in that car seat would he/she still be alive ?
